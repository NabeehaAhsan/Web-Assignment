MCS [2020]

Merit will be calculated as follows:
  1. In case of semester students, Multiply the obtained semester system marks by the Factor 0.85 to bring the marks at par with Annual System.
  2. 30% weightage for marks in matriculation examination + plus 30% weightage for marks in Intermediate Examination + 40% weightage for marks in Graduation Examination.
  3. * 10 marks would be awarded to those candidates who have studied computer as an elective subject.
  4. ** 20 marks for Hifz-e-Quran if applicable.

* & **: These marks will be added in graduation marks before calculation of % weightage.
