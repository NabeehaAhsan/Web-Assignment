PROGRAMS OFFERED @ CS

Ph.D.(CS) [NOT OFFERED for FALL 2023]
Ph.D. program is the newest program on offer at the Department of Computer Science. It leads to Ph.D degree in Computer Science.

MS(CS)
MS(CS) program is offered every two years at the Department of Computer Science. It is a two-Year Degree Program (Master of Science in Computer Science) of 30 + credit hours spread over 4 semesters (2 semesters Course work plus thesis). It is Equivalent to M.Phil (i.e. 18 years education)

MS(DS)
MS(DS) program is offered every two years at the Department of Computer Science. It is a two-Year Degree Program (Master of Science in Data Science) of 30 + credit hours spread over 4 semesters (2 semesters Course work plus thesis). It is Equivalent to M.Phil (i.e. 18 years education)

BS(CS) (Morning & Evening)
BS(CS) program is the undergraduate program offered in Morning and Evening Sessions at the Department of Computer Science. It is a four-year Degree Program (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science) of 130 credit hours spread over 8 semesters.

M.C.S. [NOT OFFERED July 2022 onwards as per HEC ADVISORY]
MCS program is a two-Year Degree Program (Master of Computer Science – Conversion Course) of 70+ credit hours spread over 4 semesters.
